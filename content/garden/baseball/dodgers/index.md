I'm a Dodgers fan.

Why? Well, as a Brit, choosing was a bit arbitrary.

I think I settled on picking a team around 2016-ish? 

My memories a little hazy...

I remember asking someone "I really want to see a really good pitcher pitch, who's the best pitcher right now?"

Someone said "Kershaw", so I started watching Dodger games.

I loved the white and blue, and I loved the history. 

Jackie Robinson

Sandy Koufax

The fact the last world series they'd won was in my birthyear (1988)

I've been a fan ever since...
