One of those nights that I still think about a lot. Brutalismus 3000 playing the Possession stage at [Boiler Room Festival London 2021](https://boilerroom.tv/artist/brutalismus-3000/), absolutely battering the room with nu-gabber and post-punk techno. I was there, sweating buckets, loving every minute.

{{< youtube pZhUS_q4jkc >}}

And yes, if you squint, you can see me in the crowd. No, I'm not going to tell you exactly where. Consider it a Where's Wally situation (with worse lighting and more black t-shirts).

A few things I remember:

- The energy was unreal. The room went from zero to absolute mayhem within about two tracks.
- "Ultrakurz" hitting live is a different beast to hearing it on record.
- I'd never been to a Boiler Room in person before and the camera getting shoved through the crowd is genuinely disorienting when you're in it.

Brutalismus went on to become properly huge after this. Bit of a "I was there" moment, I suppose. Tracklist on [1001tracklists](https://www.1001tracklists.com/tracklist/24b5m05k/brutalismus-3000-boiler-room-festival-possession-london-united-kingdom-2021-10-17.html) if you want to dig into the set.
