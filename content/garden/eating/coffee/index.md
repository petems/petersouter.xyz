Not exactly a unique trait, but I love coffee.

Overall, I'm an iced latte person: No sugar, just a little splash of milk. 

That's it.

I don't really have a complicated coffee order. I just like it cold, slightly milky, and not sweet.

I drink coffee iced all year round, even in the dead of winter. I wanna be able to drink it as soon as I order and I already run pretty hot as a bigger dude so it's always nice to have something cool.

Everyone deserves a little treat!
