The [Maotoam Hub](https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/1887649556/maotoam-worlds-first-13-in-1-usb-c-hub-and-laptop-stand) was exactly the MacBook hub I was looking for. 13-in-1, USB-C, power passthrough, Ethernet, HDMI, a laptop stand built in. Everything on the list, nothing missing, all in one device.

I still can't find anything else close to it. The best MacBook hubs I can get today usually cap out at power passthrough and HDMI, maybe a couple of USB-A ports if you're lucky. If I want Ethernet (and I do, because Wi-Fi in my office is flaky) I'm back to a dongle in a USB port, which eats one of the ports I bought the hub for in the first place.

So naturally, the one company that got the spec right no longer exists.

I backed it on Kickstarter. Some people got theirs. A lot of us didn't, and then the company quietly disappeared off the face of the internet. Classic crowdfunding story, just with my money this time.

Can someone with a factory and some spare weekends please just... copy the idea? I have cash ready. 😢
