The main inspiration for this section was [Daniel Corin's digital garden](https://www.danielcorin.com/garden/). I liked how it was a simple collection of notes organised by topic — no pressure to turn everything into a full blog post.

That felt like exactly what I needed: somewhere to put the half-formed thoughts, recommendations, and random things I want to remember or share without worrying about polish.
